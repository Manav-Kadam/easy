What We Do

We're transforming retail with autonomous checkout

Customers can walk in, grab what they need, and leave without waiting in line or stopping to scan and pay. Easy-Check’s computer vision and AI-powered solution is the only one that can be quickly and easily installed in retailers’ existing stores.

What Makes Us Different

We’re putting the customer and retail relationship first

Easy-Check’s technology is a giant leap forward for retailers who want autonomous checkout, but don’t want to build new stores to get it. Our five driving principles are:

01. Privacy

Easy-Check doesn't use any facial recognition or other biometrics. All of our deployments are on-premise to ensure maximum performance and security for retailers and their customers

02. Scalability

Easy-Check’s solution is camera-first - no turnstiles or gates. That means simple and quick installs with no disruption to customers or the business.

03. Experience

Easy-Check believes good retail is predicated on happy customers having a great experience. Customers want to find the right products and, importantly, avoid waiting in long checkout lines.

04. Flexibility

Easy-Check supports retailers’ existing environments, and can integrate with existing technology. Our installation requires primarily ceiling-mounted cameras. No need to change up your store layout to accommodate our system - we adjust to your store.

05. Insights

Analytics is in Easy-Check’s DNA. We work with retailers to understand product performance and provide real-time inventory updates.

Our Principle

AI-powered checkout should empower the shopper, not raise privacy concerns.

Easy-Check commits to protecting all of your data as well as being transparent with how we use your data.
